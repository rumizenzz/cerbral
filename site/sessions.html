Disconnect your Cerbral brain?

This signs out your Cerbral — from both Cerbral Web and Cerbral Desktop. They share one connection, so disconnecting here disconnects everywhere. Nothing in your cerbral-brain repo is deleted — your brain is yours, forever — but until you reconnect:

  • Cerbral Web can't show any sessions, memory, or instructions
  • Cerbral Desktop stops syncing new conversations to your GitHub
  • Your local data at ~/.cerbral/ on your Mac stays put — the desktop can still read it locally, but it'll no longer push to your brain-repo
