Cerbral for Mac — almost there.

The Cerbral installer is in closed beta while we finish signing and notarizing. The one-click bundle sets up your Mac in ~3 minutes:

  • Cerbral.app — the desktop client (Tauri, ~40 MB).
  • Ollama — the local AI runtime. We auto-pick the build for your chip (Apple Silicon or Intel).
  • Your AI model — tuned to your hardware. Default is Qwen3:8B (~5 GB); we'll recommend a bigger model if your Mac can handle it.
  • Local tunnel — a secure connection so Cerbral Web can reach the AI on your Mac. Nothing leaves your device without it.

Sit tight — your brain repo is safe on GitHub, and your session is waiting on /welcome. When the installer ships we'll drop it at /downloads/Cerbral.dmg and this button will just work.
